Lupus

An autoimmune disease that can affect the skin and other organs. The skin symptoms include a distinctive butterfly-shaped rash and other skin changes that can be triggered by sun exposure

Symptoms

  • Butterfly-shaped rash across cheeks and nose
  • Red, scaly patches on sun-exposed areas
  • Mouth and nose sores
  • Hair loss in patches
  • Skin sensitivity to sunlight
  • Joint pain and fatigue

It can appears in:

  • Face (butterfly rash)
  • Scalp and hairline
  • Arms and hands
  • Chest and shoulders
  • Inside mouth and nose
