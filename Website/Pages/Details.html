Angiokeratoma

A skin condition characterized by small, dark red to black bumps that may have a rough, wart-like surface due to thickened skin

Symptoms

  • Small, dark red, purple, or black papules
  • Rough or scaly surface
  • Usually not itchy but can itch or bleed
  • May appear singly or in clusters

It can appears in:

  • Private areas
  • Inner thigh
  • Lower abdomen
